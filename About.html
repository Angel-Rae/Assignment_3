About

Gohier is an artist in which specialises primarily in print making, painting and sculpting.
He was born in St Nazaire, France in 1968 and now resides In Darwin, Northern Territory.

His artwork is heavily inspired by his life and experiences residing in the Northern Territory.
Ranging in themes from politics, the habitat and environment, community, culture, history and social justice.

Goheir frequently uses bright, vibrant colours as well as popular and quirky imagery to contrast the deeper
meaning and commentary of his pieces, usually reflecting on the failiures and triumphs of society.

Goheir's work has been featured in Artbank, the Australian National Gallery Canberra, the Casula Powerhouse Art Centre,
Gold Coast Art Gallery, Griffith University, the Museum of Contemporary Art Sydney since he became a practicing artist in 1987.
